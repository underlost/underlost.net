Giving Back

For 2018, I'll be providing assistance for small organizations and other indie developers that may not be able to afford my normal rates.

Each month I'll be allocating roughly 10-15 hours of my time to assist with consulting and development. For areas of expertise and ways I may be of service, please see the Availability section.

Restrictions & Terms

I'll be offering my services at no cost to small organizations, indie developers, minorities, and any other cool and fun projects that I find interesting. Everything is on a case-by-case basis, and I reserve the right to decline my services for any reason.

How to Apply

Simply send me an email with the following information:

  • Name of your organization / group
  • A brief description of yourself / organization.
  • What you need assistance with and why you think I might be able to help
  • Any other information you may think to be pertinent (urls, examples, etc)

I'll try to respond to all enqueries within 48-72 hours. If you don't hear back, please don't take it too personal, and feel free to try again at a later date.
